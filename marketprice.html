Today's Tips for Coffee Farmers

1. Monitor market prices daily to determine the best time to sell your coffee beans. Coffee prices fluctuate based on global supply and demand.

2. During times of high market prices, it may be beneficial to sell larger quantities, but be mindful of future predictions to avoid missing out on even better prices.

3. Coffee is highly sensitive to weather conditions. Always store your harvest in a dry and cool environment to prevent deterioration and maintain the highest quality.
